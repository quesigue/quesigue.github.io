The third sentence for Que Sigue

Julka&Lulka

Posted on April 18, 2013

 

 

Yesterday, when we were travelling from Colonia to Montevideo, we met Miel, a backpacker from San Francisco. She used to travel with her boyfriend (last time they travelled through Asia), but now she says that she prefers to travel on her own. And so she is exploring South America since December, and she’s going to stay on this continent until July next year. Her stories were very inspiring for us, so we were a bit mad on ourselves when we realized that she would be the perfect person to create a new sentence for Que Sigue.

 

Fortunately, we accidently met her today walking around the old town in Montevideo. We talked a bit more  and we asked her to participate in our game. That’s what she came up with: Delightfully, some big cities have soft air with sounds that soothe and the chaos is found in the street art.

 

Miel from San Francisco writing the sentence

 

As you can see, it’s getting more poetic, but we don’t mind at all. We would like to get such a personal sentences in the future, because they show person’s attitude and it’s interesting.

 

In an instant, we’re going to Punta del Este and some other small cities by the sea. It’s time to chill out for a bit and leave the big cities behind.
